Services Offered

  • Guitar Lesson
    This services offers lesson for beginners to focus on finger placement, strumming, sight-reading, and chord placement.
  • Violin lessons
    This service offers lesson that will help customers on how to play a violin, the use of four strings, identification of the parts of a violin, and the proper posture of playing this instrument.
  • Piano/Keyboard Lesson
    This servce offers lesson for begginers to focus on learning to read notes, read rhythms, play scales, and play basic chords
  • Drum lessons
    This service offers lesson for kids/adults who wants to learn how to play the drums. We offer basic tutorials for beginners and pro tutorials for the people who wants to improve their skills.
  • Saxophone lessons
    This service offers lesson for beginners who want to learn the basics of playing saxophone like the proper mouth position, breathing, and on how to play long notes.
  • Voice Lesson
    This service offers lesson that will help customers improve their voice/singing through proper breathing, tone production, intonation, diction, phrasing, and ear training.
